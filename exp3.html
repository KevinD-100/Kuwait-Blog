FAQ

Is Kuwait expensive?
▼
No, Kuwait can be quite affordable for tourists, especially outside the peak travel seasons. While luxury accommodations and dining options exist, budget-friendly alternatives for lodging, food, and transport are also available.
What is the best time to visit Kuwait?
▼
The best time to visit Kuwait is from November to March, when the weather is cool and pleasant.
Is Kuwait a safe destination for travelers?
▼
Yes, Kuwait is generally a safe place to visit. As with any destination, it’s recommended to stay aware of your surroundings.
What currency is used in Kuwait?
▼
The official currency of Kuwait is the Kuwaiti Dinar (KWD).
Can tourists visit Kuwait’s mosques?
▼
Yes, tourists are allowed to visit certain mosques, including the Grand Mosque, which offers guided tours.
